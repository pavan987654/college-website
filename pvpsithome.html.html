About pvpsit

Self-financed Engineering College, in the state of Andhra Pradesh established in the year 1998.The Institution is highly acclaimed for Quality Assurance.

Offering 6 UG (B.Tech) Courses with intake of 720, MBA - 60.

Autonomous College by UGC since 2012

NIRF Ranking- The college is ranked among the top 200 engineering institutions in the country, and got a rank of 147 in 2020.

Strong Alumni Base- support in the form of Scholarships for academically bright and needy students, Student Research & Innovation.

The college has installed a Solar Rooftop PV System of 200 KW with an investment of nearly Rs. 1 Crore and the plant is in operation from Jan' 2018.
